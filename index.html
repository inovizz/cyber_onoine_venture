Welcome To Cyber Online Venture

End-to-end solution to conduct online and offline exams securely

What we Offer

Cyber Online Venture is a strategic unit focused on Educational Institutions and Examination Boards. It provides technology end-to-end solutions. It caters to the needs of multiple industry segments, through innovative, easy-to-use, secured, integrated solutions in a build-as-you-grow business model. Cyber Online Venture is highly modular, scalable and configurable giving businesses and educational institutions the benefits of increased efficiencies, faster go to market and better business results.

We offer an integrated suite of hardware, network, software and services and ensure that your functions are digitized, automated and connected.We offer optimal performance in normal broadband connectivity along with a stringent security mechanism.

Our model eliminates capital investment up front as we facilitate procurement of the IT infrastructure and software on rent for the duration of the contract.
